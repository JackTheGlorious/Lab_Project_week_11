Repairs and Cleaning recomended before moving out.

Repairs

Drywall: Filling or removing holes

Heating & Air: Repairing or replacing central heating and air systems

Plumbing: Fixing any leaks and replacing at risk pipes

Smoke Detectors: Ensuring smoke detectors work and have fresh batteries

Cleaning

Cleaning

Carpet: Deep Clean carpets and remove any stains

Paint: Replace broken or chipped paint

Hard Floors: Mop floors and remove scuffs and scrattches if possible

Windows: Ensure the window and blinds are clean and free of dust
